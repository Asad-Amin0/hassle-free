Abstract & Ecosystem Overview

HASSLE-FREE is an intelligent cross-platform recruitment and career enhancement ecosystem designed to transform the interaction between job seekers and employers through AI-driven automation. By integrating advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP), machine learning techniques, AI-based mock interviews, and semantic job matching algorithms into a single unified platform, it streamlines screening processes, minimizes subjective bias, and enhances overall employability.
